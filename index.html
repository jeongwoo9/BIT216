About Program Disaster

Crisis Relief Services (CRS) is an NGO (Non Government Organization) that aims to help people who are facing crises arising  from natural disasters such as flood and earthquakes. The CRS staff consists of managers and administrators, and they are governed by a Board of Directors.

Our Mission
  • Engage local churches and corporate organisations response to disasters.
  • Train local volunteers and partners in disaster management and
  • Promote disaster risk reduction among vulnerable communities.
